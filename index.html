Hi, I’m Paolo, a tenure-track assistant professor at the Center for Mind and Brain (CIMeC) and the Department of Information Engineering and Computer Science (DISI) of the University of Trento.

I obtained my Ph.D. from the University of Trento in 2015, specializing in Computer Vision perspective of Social Interaction Analysis.

After completing his Ph.D., Paolo pursued a Postdoctoral position at TU Wien from 2015 to 2017. Following that, I held another Postdoctoral position at the IIT of Genova in 2017-2018. Subsequently, I continued my academic journey as a Postdoc at the Industrial Engineering Department of the University of Trento in 2018-2019 and as RTDa at the Department of Information Engineering and Computer Science until 2022.

My research interests primarily revolve around topics related to Computer Vision, with a recent focus on Natural Language Supervision for Video Scene Understanding. I am member of the ELLIS society and CVPL.

I also co-founded Mountain Maps, an AI-powered navigator meant for mountain enthusiasts.
